Passion for adrenaline and the cold.

His heart belongs to the snowy slopes and the thrill of snowboarding. From the moment the winter starts, Gabriel's eyes light up with excitement, and he eagerly anticipates the best way to go Slovakia or Austria for his next snowboard season.

The love for snowboarding is driven by his deep connection to the cold, crisp air that fills his lungs as he carves down the slopes. He revels in the feeling of freedom that comes from gliding effortlessly over the powdery snow, the wind whipping through his hair. The chill against his cheeks is a reminder of the invigorating adventure that awaits him with each descent.

While others might shy away from the cold, Jake embraces it wholeheartedly. He knows that within the icy embrace of winter lies a world of new experiences and challenges to conquer. Whether he's perfecting his tricks in the established snowboard slopes or exploring untouched backcountry trails, Jake's thirst for adventure knows no bounds.
